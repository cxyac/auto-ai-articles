## 🎯 系统架构

### The Research Phase（研究阶段）：无人会做的事

**英文原文**：
> Before a single word of script gets written, system runs a full research sweep across the entire internet. On YouTube, it runs 15 keywords searches across three separate time-filtered passes. All time. Last 12 months. Last 30 days. For each keyword, system finds the highest-performing video, that's the ceiling, and then collects patterns downward until there's a massive drop-off in views. 1.5M to 45K? That's the floor. Stop there. The titles at the ceiling are the patterns worth stealing. Most teams do 1 search on X for what customers are saying and call it research. This system pulls hundreds, scores them, and filters automatically. On Reddit, it mines for real customer pain. And grabs exact quotes. Finds the most viral threads from the past 10 years, dives deep to find controversy, what has the most downvotes? On X, it pulls roughly 5,000 posts (via API), sorted by engagement. The same ceiling/floor logic applies. High quote tweet ratio means posts where people fought in replies. That's content with nerves. That's what we're looking for. Everything that comes out of the research phase gets indexed as ammunition for agents that come next.

**中文翻译**：
> 在脚本的单个词被写入之前，系统会在整个互联网上进行全面的研究扫描。在 YouTube 上，它在三个独立的时间过滤通道中进行 15 次关键词搜索。每次都是如此。过去 12 个月。过去 30 天。对于每个关键词，系统会找到表现最好的视频，那就是天花板，然后收集模式向下直到出现大量的浏览量下降。150 万到 4.5 万？那就是底线。就在那里停下。天花板上的标题是值得窃取的模式。大多数团队在 X 上搜索客户说什么并称之为研究。这个系统会提取数百条内容，对它们进行评分，并自动过滤。在 Reddit 上，它会挖掘真正的客户痛点。并获取精确的引用。找到过去 10 年中最具病毒性的帖子，深入挖掘以发现争议，什么拥有最多的反对票？在 X 上，它通过 API 提取大约 5,000 条帖子，按参与度排序。相同的天花板/底层逻辑适用。高引用推文比率意味着人们在回复中争论的帖子。那就是有神经质的内容。这就是我们要找的。研究阶段产出的所有内容都会被编入索引，作为后续 agent 的弹药。

### The Writing Pipeline（写作流程）：与其他 AI 工具的区别

**英文原文**：
> This is where it separates from anything else I've seen. Most AI script tools have a single agent write the whole thing. That's like hiring one person to be your researcher, copywriter, editor, and fact checker, and creative director and expecting them to do a good job. That's idiotic. Specialized agents run in sequence and each one has exactly one job. The Hook Agent: → Writes 4 hooks, each data backed by our database of proven hooks with millions of views across socials. → Every hook goes through a minimum of 3 full iterations, with a complete diagnosis of what's weak before it gets rewritten. → Then, Hook Manager scores it across 5 dimensions and everything has to hit 10/10. It doesn't pass? We sent it back and Hook Agent rewrites it. Nothing moves forward until it clears the gate. The same structure runs for body, for CTA, for everything. The agents don't just write. They each have a boss who won't let anything through that isn't ready.

**中文翻译**：
> 这是它与我见过的任何其他东西不同的地方。大多数 AI 脚本工具有一个 agent 写整件事。这就像雇佣一个人做研究员、文案、编辑、事实检查员和创意总监，并期望他们做好工作。这太愚蠢了。专业的 agent 按顺序运行，每个只有一项工作。Hook Agent（钩子代理）→ 撰写 4 个钩子，每个钩子都由我们数据库中数百万观看次数的经过验证的钩子支持。→ 每个钩子至少经过 3 次完整迭代，在重写之前完整诊断出弱点。→ 然后，Hook Manager（钩子管理器）在 5 个维度上对它进行评分，所有内容必须达到 10/10。它不通过吗？我们把它发回，Hook Agent 重写它。在它清空大门之前没有任何东西向前推进。相同的结构运行正文、CTA 以及所有内容。这些 agent 不只是写。它们每个人都有一个老板，不会让任何未准备好通过的东西通过。

### The Weapons Check（武器检查）：质量控制

**英文原文**：
> This is the part I think about most when I explain this system to people. Every single line in script gets scored on two dimensions independently. → Invention Novelty: does this line make the product feel like a genuine breakthrough? → Copy Intensity: is it sharp enough that someone reading it actually feels something, not just understands something? Both have to hit 10/10. A novel idea with flat copy fails. Sharp copy about a boring feature fails. Lines that don't pass get rewritten. Lines that are pure filler with no possible weapon version get cut entirely. Character budgets are hard enforced throughout whole pipeline. Every line earns its place or it gets removed. This is why our scripts feel different from what most founders end up with. The average launch script has 3 great lines and 40 seconds of filler. Founder stumbles through awkwardly on camera. Every line in ours survived a weapons check.

**中文翻译**：
> 这是我在向人们解释这个系统时想到最多的部分。脚本中的每一行都在两个维度上独立评分。→ 创新新颖性：这一行是否让产品感觉像一个真正的突破？→ 复制强度：它是否足够犀利，以至于读它的人实际上感觉到了某种东西，而不仅仅是理解了什么？两者都必须达到 10/10。带有平淡复制的创新想法会失败。关于无聊功能的犀利复制会失败。无法通过被重写的行会被完全切断。没有可能武器版本的纯填充行会被完全删除。字符预算在整个流程中强制执行。每一行赢得它的位置或被删除。这就是为什么我们的脚本感觉与大多数创始人最终呈现的不同。平均发布脚本有 3 个好行和 40 秒的填充内容。创始人在摄像机上笨拙地绊倒。在我们脚本中的每一行都通过了武器检查。

### The Output（输出）：三标签页输出

**英文原文**：
> The whole thing lands in a Google Doc with three tabs. Research, Working Script, and Final Script. → The Research tab has everything pulled across YouTube, Reddit, and X. → The Working Script tab shows every iteration, diagnosis, and rewrite from every agent so you have a full paper trail. → The Final Script tab is clean and copy paste ready with 4 hook options and 2 CTA options. From there humans go through each line tactically and edit until perfection. These agents are trained on our writing, giving it a true advantage over other AI writing tools and general copywriting agents.

**中文翻译**：
> 整个东西落在一个 Google 文档中，有三个标签页。研究、工作脚本和最终脚本。→ 研究标签页有从 YouTube、Reddit 和 X 中提取的所有内容。→ 工作脚本标签页展示每次迭代、诊断和来自每个 agent 的重写，这样你就有了完整的论文轨迹。→ 最终脚本标签页是干净、复制粘贴就绪的，有 4 个钩子选项和 2 个 CTA 选项。从那里开始，人类会策略性地编辑每一行直到完美。这些 agent 经过我们文案训练，相比其他 AI 写作工具和通用文案写作 agent，给予了真正的优势。
